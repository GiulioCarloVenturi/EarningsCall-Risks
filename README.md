# EARNINGs CALL RISKS

## Goal

Evaluate risks flagged by public companies in earning calls.

## Content

1. Retrieve earnings call reports from NL Analytics for risk factors: COVID-19, Financing Conditions, Monetary PolicY, Supply Chain, Inflation, Ukraine, Middle East, Tariffs, exchange rate  
2. Process reports to extract the fraction of risk senteces associated to each risk factor
3. Plot a risk index for the selected risk factors
