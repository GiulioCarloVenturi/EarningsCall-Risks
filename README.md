# EARNING CALLS RISKS
## Goal
Evaluate risks flagged by public companies in earning calls.
## Content
1. Retrieve earning call reports from NL Analytics for 9 risk factors
2. Process reports to extract the fraction of risk senteces associated to each risk factor
3. Plot a risk index for the selected risk factors ![alt text](https://github.com/GiulioCarloVenturi/EarningsCall-Risks/blob/main/NLAnalytics_Risk_EAUS.png)
